About FRAGRANCE OBSESSION

Welcome to FRAGRANCE OBSESSION, your premier destination for luxury perfumes and exclusive fragrances. Established with the mission to bring timeless elegance to your everyday life, we curate the finest scents from around the world.

At FRAGRANCE OBSESSION, we believe that a scent is more than just an accessory—it’s a reflection of personality, a whisper of your story, and a celebration of individuality. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and quality products to our valued customers.





How It All Began

Our Founder's journey began in her young adulthood years. She had a profound passion for beauty, but found it hard to purchase good-quality products. It was this reason that made her determined to find a solution, and this is how FO Cosmetics was born. In the constantly changing landscape of beauty, FO Cosmetic stands as the pioneering force in Kosovo. We’re proud to be the top online beauty destination, a trusted distributor of fragrances, skincare, makeup, hair-care items, home & lifestyle products being able to satisfy the needs of unique preferences of beauty enthusiasts, no matter the age, gender, orientation and background. At FO Cosmetics, we're not just a business. We're the result of our Founder's passionate vision. Our story is one of transformation - it began with a young woman looking for better options and became a platform that empowers beauty lovers in Kosovo and beyond.


Our Mission

As the e-commerce company dedicated exclusively to beauty products in Kosovo, our mission at FO Cosmetic is characterised by a clear a dedication to our goals. Our primary goal has always been to provide beauty enthusiasts with premium products. We are committed to offering a curated selection of fragrances, skincare, makeup, hair-care items, home & lifestyle products that cater to the differente preferences of our customers. Our journey began with the vision of offering accessibility to high-quality beauty products, and today, that vision continues to drive us forward. In addition to our commitment to quality, our vision extends to a broader mission – one where we aspire to introduce global trends in marketing, youth interests, and beauty to Kosovo. FO Cosmetic is the lider, often being the only one provider of newly released beauty products. Our mission, therefore, extends beyond the borders of Kosovo, as we work hard to bring the latest global beauty trends and innovations to our homeland.
